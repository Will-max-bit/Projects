To book with an artist, please read the booking info page and email an artist directly for all tattoo inquiries.

booking info
Due to the custom nature of our shop, we do not have an admin or office manager who books appointments. Each artist accepts work at different volumes. While some take emails year round, several of our artists only open their books to requests for work in a specific time window every few months, in order to condense their correspondence times. Each artist has details of how they accept inquiries on their artist page. If the artist’s page that you are interested in says “books are closed”, that means that you cannot request an appointment now, and will have to wait for the next time window when that artist is booking. If you are interested in an artist whose books are currently closed, we recommend following them on social media through the links on their artist page to be notified exactly when their books open. Our artists schedule their own consultations and appointments personally, so contacting them as is outlined on their artist’s page is the only way they can be reached. If you are not particular about which tattoo artist does your work, and do not want to wait for our artist’s books to open, we suggest visiting any of the other tattoo shops in the area, as nearly all of them take walk-ins and are of good quality.
